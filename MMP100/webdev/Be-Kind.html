Empathy and Compassion Go a Long Way!
By listening to others,

engaging in acts of service,

observing the empathetic actions of others,

and imagining yourself in another person's situation,

are all strategies that can help build empathy.

So, pick up your mobile device and
call 5 friends or family members everyday

to check on their well-being.
Together, we can beat this by staying apart!
